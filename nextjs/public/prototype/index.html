TRAVEL GENTLY

Mountain comfort rules

  • Pause for a restroom or refreshment break every 2–2.5 hours where possible.
  • Protect a 45–60 minute rest window after long transfers.
  • Do not overexert at altitude; start early and descend if unwell.
  • Respect monasteries, local customs and restricted military areas.
  • Carry water, identity documents and offline contact details.
